Keith Elliot

Platform Issues


Every county and district goes through change. Keith Elliot has seen how certain changes implement the community at large in Sully District, and is planning to do something about it. He has changes that would benefit the entire public at large, using his qualifications to help him lead his county to a brighter future.

Taxes


Keith Elliot wishes to be as cost-efficient as possible by thoroughly analyzing the budget and discarding anything that proves to be unnecessary or unused. He believes that tax rates should be greatly slowed and will attempt to maximize revenue in other ways. He believes that if any tax is found non-essential, it should be removed.

Schools


Teachers are essential to Fairfax County's future. Keith Elliot wishes to attract some of the best teachers to Fairfax County as well as increase their pay for their hard work. He will help allow parents to have their opinions heard about their kids schooling and any other activities related to their children.

Law Enforcement


Keith wishes to raise awareness about law enforcement officers as well as encourage people to join law enforcement. He will provide law enforcement the necessary tools as well as raising their salary for their hard work. Keith wants law enforcement to be able to safely enter and protect the citizens of fairfax.

Zoning ordinance of Fairfax County


The Zoning Ordinance of Fairfax wishes to take away single-family houses and replace them with apartments, to create more living space. Keith Elliot opposes this because it will cause overcrowding in schools already filled to the brim and will increase traffic in areas where people may have to travel to get to work.

County owned land


Many counties reserve land for its plentiful supply of resources. However, Keith believes that a lot of these can be found elsewhere, rendering the land useless. With the increasing population, Keith proposes that land owned by the county be converted into land for people to live.

Burdening Regulations


Regulations are used to ensure that a country or place works properly and efficiently for everyone. However, several regulations can cross the line and prohibit people from performing specific activities. Keith will rid these burdensome regulations to ensure a free and prosperous county.
